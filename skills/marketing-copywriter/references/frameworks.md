## Headline Formulas (50+ Proven Templates)

### Category 1: How-To Headlines

```
1. "How to [Desirable Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
   Example: "How to Lose 20 Pounds in 30 Days"

2. "How to [Achieve Goal] Without [Common Pain]"
   Example: "How to Build Muscle Without Spending Hours in the Gym"

3. "How [Type of Person] [Achieve Outcome]"
   Example: "How Busy Moms Earn $5K/Month from Home"

4. "How to Get [Benefit] Even If [Objection]"
   Example: "How to Get Fit Even If You Hate Exercise"

5. "How to [Do Something] Like [Aspirational Person/Group]"
   Example: "How to Invest Like Warren Buffett"
```

### Category 2: Curiosity Headlines

```
6. "The Secret to [Desirable Outcome]"
   Example: "The Secret to Effortless Weight Loss"

7. "What [Expert/Group] Don't Want You to Know About [Topic]"
   Example: "What Banks Don't Want You to Know About Credit Scores"

8. "The [Number] [Things] That [Outcome]"
   Example: "The 7 Foods That Burn Belly Fat"

9. "Little Known Ways to [Achieve Outcome]"
   Example: "Little Known Ways to Double Your Productivity"

10. "[Number] [Type of Person] Who [Achievement]"
    Example: "7 Entrepreneurs Who Built Million-Dollar Businesses in Their Garage"
```

### Category 3: Question Headlines

```
11. "Do You Make These Mistakes in [Area]?"
    Example: "Do You Make These Mistakes in English?"

12. "Are You [Negative Situation]?"
    Example: "Are You Leaving Money on the Table?"

13. "What Would You Do If [Desirable Scenario]?"
    Example: "What Would You Do If You Never Had to Worry About Money?"

14. "Why Do [Group] [Behavior/Outcome]?"
    Example: "Why Do Smart Investors Avoid These 3 Stocks?"

15. "Which Type of [Person/Thing] Are You?"
    Example: "Which Type of Entrepreneur Are You?"
```

### Category 4: Warning/Negative Headlines

```
16. "Warning: [Negative Consequence]"
    Example: "Warning: Your Website Is Costing You Thousands"

17. "[Number] Mistakes That [Negative Outcome]"
    Example: "5 Mistakes That Kill Your Job Interview"

18. "Never [Do Action] Until You [Condition]"
    Example: "Never Buy a Car Until You Read This"

19. "Stop [Negative Behavior] and Start [Positive Behavior]"
    Example: "Stop Wasting Time on Social Media and Start Building Your Business"

20. "Don't [Action] Unless [Condition]"
    Example: "Don't Launch Your Product Unless You've Done This"
```

### Category 5: Benefit-Driven Headlines

```
21. "[Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
    Example: "Speak Spanish Fluently in 90 Days"

22. "Get [Benefit] Without [Pain/Effort]"
    Example: "Get Six-Pack Abs Without Doing Crunches"

23. "[Number]-[Unit] [Product/Method] That [Outcome]"
    Example: "30-Second Morning Ritual That Boosts Energy All Day"

24. "Finally, [Solution] for [Problem]"
    Example: "Finally, a CRM That Actually Saves You Time"

25. "The Fast Way to [Outcome]"
    Example: "The Fast Way to Learn Piano"
```

### Category 6: Proof & Credibility Headlines

```
26. "[Number] Proven Ways to [Outcome]"
    Example: "11 Proven Ways to Increase Email Open Rates"

27. "Scientific Discovery: [Finding]"
    Example: "Scientific Discovery: This Nutrient Reverses Aging"

28. "[Number]% of [Group] [Do Something]. Here's Why"
    Example: "87% of Millionaires Read This Book. Here's Why"

29. "Tested: [Claim]"
    Example: "Tested: This Ad Pulled 340% Better Than Our Control"

30. "Case Study: How [Person/Company] [Achievement]"
    Example: "Case Study: How Sarah Went from $0 to $100K in 6 Months"

### Category 7: News & Announcement Headlines

```
31. "Announcing: [New Thing]"
    Example: "Announcing: The World's First AI-Powered Sleep Tracker"

32. "At Last: [Solution] for [Problem]"
    Example: "At Last: A Pain Relief Method That Actually Works"

33. "New: [Product/Method] [Benefit]"

   grow their following by 400%+"

✓ Clear outcome 3

Clear next step
→ "Tap 'Learn More' to get the free guide
   (before we take it down Friday)"

## Objection Handling Framework

### Common Objections & Responses

```
╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║         OBJECTION HANDLING TEMPLATE           ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════╝

OBJECTION: "It's too expensive"
RESPONSE:
"I understand. Let me put this in perspective:
You're currently [losing X / spending Y] because
of [problem]. This solution pays for itself in
[timeframe] and then becomes pure profit/savings.
The real question is: can you afford NOT to fix this?"

────────────────────────────────────────────────

OBJECTION: "I don't have time"
RESPONSE:
"That's exactly why you need this. It takes just
[small time] to set up, then it saves you [larger time]
every [period]. You'll actually GET time back, not
lose it. The setup is [timeframe]—less time than
you'll spend [comparison activity] this week."

────────────────────────────────────────────────

OBJECTION: "I need to think about it"
RESPONSE:
"Absolutely. Let me ask: what specific questions
do you still have? [Address]. The only reason to
wait is if [valid reason]. Otherwise, every day
you delay costs you [specific loss]. Here's a
30-day guarantee, so there's zero risk."

────────────────────────────────────────────────

OBJECTION: "Will this work for me?"
RESPONSE:
"Great question. This works for [type of people]
who [situation/goal]. Specifically, you'll see
results if you [simple criteria]. [Social proof
example of someone like them]. Plus, you're
protected by [guarantee], so you can verify
it works with zero risk."

────────────────────────────────────────────────

OBJECTION: "I've tried similar things before"
RESPONSE:
"I hear you. Most [solutions] fail because [reason].
That's exactly what makes this different. Instead
of [old way], this uses [unique mechanism] which
solves the root problem. Here's the proof: [specific
differentiator + social proof]."
```

## Checklist: Copy Quality Control

```
Before publishing any copy, verify:

HEADLINE & HOOK
☐ Grabs attention in first 3 seconds
☐ Clearly states benefit or creates curiosity
☐ Speaks to target audience specifically
☐ Includes power words where appropriate

STRUCTURE
☐ Opening hooks immediately
☐ Flows logically (problem → solution → proof → offer)
☐ Varied sentence length for rhythm
☐ Clear sections with subheadings
☐ White space for readability

PERSUASION ELEMENTS
☐ Specific, not generic claims
☐ Emotional triggers present
☐ Logical justification provided
☐ Social proof included
☐ Unique mechanism explained
☐ Risk reversal (guarantee)
☐ Urgency/scarcity created
☐ Objections pre-handled

CTA
☐ Single, clear action
☐ Benefit-focused button text
☐ Urgency included
☐ Friction removed
☐ Repeated at key points

TONE & VOICE
☐ "You" language throughout
☐ Conversational, not corporate
☐ Authentic, not salesy
☐ Appropriate for audience sophistication
☐ Grammar and spelling perfect

PROOF & CREDIBILITY
☐ Specific numbers, not vague claims
☐ Testimonials with real names/details
☐ Authority indicators present
☐ Results quantified
☐ Before/after shown

MOBILE OPTIMIZATION
☐ Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
☐ Scannable with bullet points
☐ CTA visible and tappable
☐ Key points above the fold
```
