## Caption Formula Shortcuts

### The 3-Line Punch
```
Line 1: [Hook question or bold statement]
Line 2: [Pain point or desire]
Line 3: [CTA with benefit]
```

### The P.A.S. Formula
```
Problem: [What they're struggling with]
Agitate: [Why it's frustrating/painful]
Solve: [Your product as the solution]
```

### The Feel-Felt-Found Method
```
"I know how you feel: [empathy with problem]

I felt the same way: [share your experience]

But then I found: [your solution/product]"
```

### The 5-Step Engagement Driver
```
1. Hook (question/statement)
2. Relate (share similar experience)
3. Educate (provide value/insight)
4. Offer (present product/solution)
5. Engage (ask question/prompt action)
```
